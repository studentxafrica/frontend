STUDENT_X LIMITED – DATA CONSENT FORM

Last updated: October 2025

1. Introduction

At Student_X Limited (“we,” “our,” or “us”), we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data in compliance with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019.
This Consent Form explains what personal information we collect, how we use it, and your rights as a data subject.

By confirming consent digitally, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to the collection and processing of your data as outlined below.

2. Information We Collect

To verify your student status and provide access to StudentX services, we may collect, but are not limited to, the following information:

  • Full name
  • Personal and/or campus email address
  • Student ID number
  • Name of university or college
  • Proof of enrollment (e.g., photo of student ID)
  • Platform usage and interaction data (e.g., activity, searches, clicks)

We may update the categories of information collected as our platform evolves, always in line with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019.

3. Purpose of Data Collection

Your information is collected and processed for the following purposes:

  • To verify your student status and create your StudentX account
  • To provide you with access to discounts, offers, and verified student services
  • To improve the platform experience and analyze general usage trends
  • To generate aggregated, anonymized insights for business and vendor analytics
  • To communicate relevant updates, offers, and service notifications

Your data will never be sold, rented, or shared with third parties without your consent, except where required by law.

4. Data Protection and Security

We use secure systems and appropriate technical and organizational safeguards to ensure your personal information is protected from unauthorized access, alteration, loss, or disclosure.
However, you understand that no online system can be guaranteed 100 % secure, and you agree to use the platform at your own discretion.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data will be retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described above or as required by law.
You have the right to withdraw your consent or request deletion of your personal information at any time by contacting info@studentx.co.ke.
Upon such a request, we will review and, where applicable, delete or anonymize your personal data in compliance with the Kenya Data Protection Act, 2019.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and request a copy of your personal data;
  • Request correction of inaccurate or outdated information;
  • Withdraw consent to data processing;
  • Request deletion or restriction of your personal data;
  • Lodge a complaint with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC) if you believe your rights have been violated.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact info@studentx.co.ke.

7. Declaration of Consent

By checking the box or selecting “I Agree” during registration, you confirm that you:

  • Have read and understood this Data Consent Form;
  • Voluntarily provide your information for registration, verification, and service use on the StudentX platform;
  • Understand that you can withdraw your consent at any time;
  • Agree to the terms outlined in this Consent Form and the accompanying Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions of Student_X Limited.
